About Me

My name is Randy and I am orginally from the state of Nevada and I grew up in Reno graduating from Reno High School. After attending Truckee Meadows Community College, I decided to make something from my life and I decided to join the United States Navy. I chose the medical field and I became a Navy Medical Corpsman, because I felt the need to serve others and take care of the sick. Although I enjoyed serving in the Navy I decided to get complete my military contract and I transitioned to civilian life and began my career in the nursing field as a Licensed Vocational Nurse-LVN.

After working several years working as an LVN, I enrolled in the University of Phoenix and I completed my Bachelor's Degree in Business Management seeking to enter the business world. I became employed by Bank One Corporation working as an Operations Associate processing business deposits in the lockbox industry utilizing automated data entry technology. I became interested in the computer field and I decided to pursue my passions as a web developer by enrolling at UCLA Bootcamp extension course.
